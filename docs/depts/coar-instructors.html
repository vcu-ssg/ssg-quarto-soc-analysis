Instructor Teaching Activity - COAR

Published

August 6, 2025

Instructor teaching activity for COAR.

Overview

This report slices and dices instructor teaching activity several ways:

  • Recent academic years - a listing of instructor activity for a recent academic year for instructors teaching during that academic year.

  • All instructors - a listing of instructor activity over time for all instructors in the sample.

The page navigation on the right side of the display can help as you explore the data.

Issues and Errors

Banner doesn’t always reflect reality. Contact John Leonard if you find inconsistences and we’ll incorporate them into the next iteration of the report. See this section to learn more.
